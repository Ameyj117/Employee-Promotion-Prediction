Promotion Prediction Using ML


Our project leverages machine learning to predict employee promotions, providing organizations with valuable insights into career advancement opportunities. By analyzing various factors such as departmental performance, education, training history, and individual achievements, our model predicts the likelihood of an employee's promotion. This data-driven approach helps companies identify high-potential employees, optimize their talent management strategies, and foster a more motivated and effective workforce. Embracing this technology can enhance decision-making processes and support career development initiatives within the organization.
